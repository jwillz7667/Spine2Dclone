## SCENE 2 (0:20 - 0:55) SUNNY MEADOW PARK

Picnic blanket, basket, red wagon with a contraption on it (the Fetch-O-Matic).
MUSIC `meadow` (light, playful). Ambience SFX `birds-meadow`.
Gunner and Beans play tug-of-war with a striped rope. Gunner is planted like a
statue; Beans is flailing. Luna cranks her gadget. SFX `tug-growl`, `crank-ratchet`.

- B-201 BEANS: "I got it! I got it! ...I do not got it!"
  (Beans loses grip, tumbles backward, SFX `boing-tumble`)
- G-202 GUNNER: "Nice try, Beans! One more round?"
- L-203 LUNA: "Or... you could test my new invention! The Fetch-O-Matic three thousand!"
  (She pats the wagon catapult. It creaks. SFX `creak-gadget`)

Pip dive-bombs in and lands on the basket. SFX `flap-land`.

- P-204 PIP: "Big news, big news! Mama Duck's eggs hatched! Three fuzzy ducklings, right down at Willow Creek!"
- G-205 GUNNER: "Ducklings? Picnic at the creek! Team Gunner, roll out!"
  (All cheer and dash off frame right. Gunner grabs the rope and wears it like a
  bandolier. PLANT: the rope goes with them. SFX `whoosh-group`)
- B-206 BEANS: "Wait for meee!"

Cut to:

## SCENE 3 (0:55 - 1:30) WILLOW CREEK BANK

Gentle creek, willow tree, lily pads. Mama Duck swims with three ducklings in a
row. MUSIC `meadow` continues softer. Ambience SFX `creek-flow`.
The team watches from the bank, hearts in their eyes. SFX `quack-mama`, `quack-babies`.

- L-301 LUNA: "Aww. They are so tiny."
- G-302 GUNNER: "Almost as tiny as Beans."
- B-303 BEANS: "Hey! I am travel sized."

A GUST hits. SFX `wind-gust`. The picnic's inflatable donut float blows off the
bank and lands in the creek. The three ducklings hop onto it like a trampoline.
SFX `boing-soft` x3, `quack-babies`. The current catches the float. It drifts.
Mama Duck flaps in alarm. SFX `quack-alarm`, `flap-panic`. MUSIC `tension-rise` sting.

- P-304 PIP: "Uh oh. Uh OH! The ducklings are floating away!"
- G-305 GUNNER: "Duckling overboard! Team, follow that float!"

MUSIC `chase` starts (driving, fun). Cut to:

## SCENE 4 (1:30 - 2:10) THE CHASE / PIP'S TRY

Side-scrolling run along the bank, background parallax. Everyone in run cycles.
SFX `paws-running` loop under. The float bobs ahead midstream. SFX `river-fast`.

- P-401 PIP: "I got this! Pigeon power!"

Pip zooms to the float, grabs the valve stem with his feet, strains upward.
The float does not move an inch. His face goes red. A feather pops off. SFX
`strain-squeak`, `feather-pop`.

- P-402 PIP: "Too... heavy... abort, abort!"

Pip flops back onto Gunner's head, deflated. SFX `flop-soft`.

- G-403 GUNNER: "You okay, Pip?"
- P-404 PIP: "The float is fine. My pride? Soggy."
- L-405 LUNA: "The creek bends at the old log! We can cut them off!"

Cut to:

## SCENE 5 (2:10 - 2:50) OLD LOG BEND / LUNA'S TRY

A fallen log crosses half the creek at a bend. The team skids in. SFX `skid`.
Luna wheels the Fetch-O-Matic to the bank and cranks it. SFX `crank-ratchet`.

- L-501 LUNA: "Fetch-O-Matic... launch the bridge!"

TWANG. SFX `catapult-twang`. A long branch arcs and lands from the log to a rock,
completing a bridge. SFX `branch-thunk`. MUSIC `tension-rise`.

- B-502 BEANS: "A bridge! I will grab them! Tiny paws, do not fail me now!"

Beans scampers onto the branch... and freezes halfway, shivering, eyes huge.
SFX `wobble-creak`, `shiver-rattle`.

- B-503 BEANS: "So. Much. Water."
- G-504 GUNNER: "Hang on, buddy! I got you!"

The float with the ducklings sweeps UNDER the branch and past. The branch snaps.
SFX `crack-snap`, `splash-big`. Gunner snags Beans by the collar mid-fall and
yanks him back to the bank. SFX `yank-grab`. Beans drips. Beat.

- L-505 LUNA: "They are heading for Fog Hollow. We will never see them in there!"

MUSIC `chase` resumes urgent. Cut to:

## SCENE 6 (2:50 - 3:20) FOG HOLLOW / THE TURN

Thick fog over the creek. Gray-blue light, soft shapes. Ambience SFX `fog-wind`.
MUSIC `lost` (slow, minor, gentle). The team creeps along the bank, squinting.

- G-601 GUNNER: "I can't see a thing."
- B-602 BEANS: "This is all my fault. My paws were too tiny. My bark is too loud..."
- G-603 GUNNER: "Too loud? Beans... that's it! Your bark is not too loud. It is JUST loud enough!"
- B-604 BEANS: "You mean it?"
- G-605 GUNNER: "Bark, buddy. Bark like you mean it!"

Beans plants his paws, inhales until he doubles in size, and MEGA BARKS.
SFX `mega-bark` with echo. The fog itself ripples. A beat... then distant quacks
answer from screen left. SFX `quack-distant`. MUSIC `hope-sting`.

- L-606 LUNA: "The quacks came from the left! This way!"

MUSIC `chase-final` (biggest version). Cut to:

## SCENE 7 (3:20 - 4:15) WATERFALL POINT / THE RESCUE

The creek widens. A WATERFALL edge cuts the frame right. Mist. SFX `waterfall-roar`.
The float with three terrified ducklings drifts toward the drop. The team bursts
from the fog onto the last spit of bank.

- P-701 PIP: "Waterfall! Dead ahead!"
- G-702 GUNNER: "Team Gunner, huddle up! Luna, tie your best knot. Pip, fly the loop to the float. Beans, count us down!"

FAST MONTAGE: Luna whips the striped rope off Gunner's shoulder and ties a loop.
SFX `rope-whip`. Pip grabs the loop, rockets out over the water. SFX `flap-fast`.
He hooks it over the float's valve stem. SFX `hook-click`. Gunner wraps the rope
around his chest, digs all four paws into the mud. SFX `paw-dig`.

- L-703 LUNA: "Double sailor knot. Done!"
- P-704 PIP: "Loop is on! Go, go, go!"
- B-705 BEANS: "Three! Two! One! TUG!"

Gunner PULLS. The rope goes piano-wire tight. SFX `rope-stretch`. The float stops
DEAD at the waterfall lip, spray flying. Slow-mo strain, Gunner sliding, teeth
gritted. MUSIC `climax`.

- G-706 GUNNER: "Little legs..."
- G-707 GUNNER: "BIG! HEART!"

One giant heave. SFX `heave-yank`. The float shoots back upstream and beaches on
the bank. The ducklings tumble out onto the grass, dizzy and safe. SFX
`slide-thump`, `quack-babies`. Mama Duck rushes in, wings wide. SFX `quack-mama`,
`flap-land`. MUSIC `victory`.

- L-708 LUNA: "You did it, Gunner!"
- G-709 GUNNER: "WE did it. All of us."

Cut to:

## SCENE 8 (4:15 - 4:45) GOLDEN HOUR PICNIC

Back at the meadow, sunset colors, the picnic finally happening. Everyone flopped
and happy, ducklings wearing tiny leaf party hats. MUSIC `golden` (warm, soft).
Ambience SFX `birds-evening`.

- P-801 PIP: "Extra, extra! Team Gunner saves the day!"
- B-802 BEANS: "And nobody got soggy! ...Except Pip."
- P-803 PIP: "My pride dried fast."
- G-804 GUNNER: "See? You do not have to be big to save the day. You just have to be there for your friends."
- L-805 LUNA: "And always bring a good rope."

The three ducklings waddle over and strike Gunner's hero pose in a tiny row.
SFX `quack-squeak`, `pop-cute`.

- G-806 GUNNER: "Aww. Welcome to the team."

## SCENE 9 (4:45 - 5:00) THE END

MUSIC `theme-reprise`. Iris-in closes on Gunner as he winks. SFX `wink-ting`,
`iris-pop`. "The End" card.

- G-901 GUNNER: "See you next time, pups!"

A duckling pops its head through the iris hole. SFX `quack-squeak`. Iris snaps
shut. MUSIC button. BLACK.
